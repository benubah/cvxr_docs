Questions

  1. Where can I go for help?
  2. My problem is not DCP compliant? Help!
  3. I want to report a bug.

Answers

  1. Where can I go for help?

Please use the Stackoverflow cvx forum for posting questions.

  1. My problem is not DCP compliant? Help!

Your best bet is the reference Boyd and Vandenberghe (2004) book. It is just not possible for us to analyze every individual problem and determine how to reformulate it in DCP form.

  1. I want to report a bug.

Please open an issue on Github Issues and include a reproducible example.

Boyd, S., and L. Vandenberghe. 2004. Convex Optimization. Cambridge University Press.
